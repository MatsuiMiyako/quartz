
# Electrolyte Conductivity Investigation
### Chemistry 30S Final Project

---

> **Inquiry Question:** How does the electrolyte concentration of different sports drinks affect their electrical conductivity, and what does this reveal about their effectiveness as electrolyte replacements?

---

## What This Project Is About

When you sweat, your body loses more than just water — it loses **electrolytes**: charged mineral ions like sodium (Na⁺), potassium (K⁺), and chloride (Cl⁻) that keep your muscles and nerves working. Sports drinks like Gatorade and Powerade are designed to replace these ions.

But do they all work equally well? And could a simple homemade solution do the same job?

In this project, I built a conductivity tester using SNAP circuits and tested five different drinks to find out which one contains the most electrolytes — and which gives you the most value.
